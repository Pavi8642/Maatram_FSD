Ideas are worth spreading

Hannah,lan & Leon 7 thgrade

WELCOME TO
ED SCHOOL

A message from a teacher to students that can motivate them to learn and set goals for the year. A good message can highlight the value of education and the students' contributions.

PRACTICAL SCHOOL

A practical school is a school that focuses on hands-on learning and real-world skills. Practical learning can help students develop time management skills, learn to work independently, and gain experience with concepts that they might encounter in a job.

SENSORIAL ACTIVIES

The Sensorial activities play a crucial role in the development of the whole child. Children learn to perceive, interact, and experience the world around them.

LANGUAGE,SCIENCE...

The scientific study of language is called linguistics. It's the study of how humans use language to communicate and organize their thoughts. Linguistics has many branches and subfields, and can be studied from a variety of perspectives.
