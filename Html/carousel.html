Bootstrap

Bootstrap is a powerful front-end framework used for designing and developing responsive and mobile-first websites. It was originally developed by Twitter and is now an open-source framework maintained by the Bootstrap community. It simplifies the process of creating web designs by providing pre-styled components and a grid system.

Blog

A blog is a type of website or online platform where individuals or groups share content, ideas, experiences, or expertise regularly. Blogs are often written in an informal or conversational tone, and their topics can range widely, including personal interests, hobbies, professional insights, news, reviews, tutorials, and more.
