Together We Can Save Lives

A non-governmental organization (NGO) is defined as a non-profit organization, group or institution that operates independently from a Government and has humanitarian or development objectives.The purpose of an NGO is to focus on all topics relating to human rights, social issues, environmental issues, and advocacy. They strive to develop and improve society's social and political conditions on a large scale.
